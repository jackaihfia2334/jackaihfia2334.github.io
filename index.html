Linear Algebra study plan (by MIT)

Introduction to the course

The planned course are “Linear Algebra” and “Matrix Methods in Data Analysis, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning”, MIT Course Number: 18.06 / 18.065. This is a course offered by Prof. Gilbert Strang.Through these two courses, you will deeply understand the nature of linear algebra and master the basic matrix methods required in research fields such as machine learning.
More info: 18.06
More info: 18.065

Study plan

There are a total of 70 lectures in the two courses, and the second course will be the main one. The first course will be reviewed in the forgetting place. Complete a lecture in two days, and extract notes and complete the exercises. The integrated data will be uploaded to this blog or Github

my first blog

This is my first blog, writing for testing and commemoration. In the future, I will record my learning process in this way, and share my experience. It is hoped that this way of forced output will enhance the learning effect and at the same time add a sense of ritual to learning. Of course, this is also cool!
One thing needs to be stated, this blog will use English as much as possible, and also use Chinese to explain if necessary.This is not only because English is the universal natural language of the world, just like the status of mathematics in the natural sciences, but also to improve my English writing ability and make the interface more concise.
The content of the blog will focus on computer science and mathematics, while also sharing some interesting and practical resources. While enjoying the great convenience brought by the Internet, I am naturally happy to share my acquirement. As the saying goes, exploring truth is more valuable than possessing truth.
It will update from time to time, but I will keep hard and try my best to do it.
