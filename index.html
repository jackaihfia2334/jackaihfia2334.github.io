The planned course are “Linear Algebra” and “Matrix Methods in Data Analysis, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning”, MIT Course Number: 18.06 / 18.065. This is a course offered by Prof. Gilbert Strang.Through these two courses, you will deeply understand the nature of linear algebra and master the basic matrix methods required in research fields such as machine learning.
More info: 18.06
More info: 18.065
