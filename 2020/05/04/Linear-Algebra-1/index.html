Linear Algebra study plan (by MIT)

Introduction to the course

The planned course are “Linear Algebra” and “Matrix Methods in Data Analysis, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning”, MIT Course Number: 18.06 / 18.065. This is a course offered by Prof. Gilbert Strang.Through these two courses, you will deeply understand the nature of linear algebra and master the basic matrix methods required in research fields such as machine learning.
More info: 18.06
More info: 18.065

Study plan

There are a total of 70 lectures in the two courses, and the second course will be the main one. The first course will be reviewed in the forgetting place. Complete a lecture in two days, and extract notes and complete the exercises. The integrated data will be uploaded to this blog or Github
